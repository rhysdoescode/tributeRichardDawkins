Richard Dawkins in nature

Richard Dawkins is a famous biologist and evolutionist. He his probably most famous for his books, including The God Delusion andThe Selfish Gene, and is critical of the idea of intelligent design and creationism; frequently discussing these topics during his debates and books.
Intrestingly, he invented the term 'meme', in The Selfish Gene.

For more information, visit his Wikipedia Page.
